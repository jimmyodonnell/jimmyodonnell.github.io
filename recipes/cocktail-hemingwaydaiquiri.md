### Ingredients

| ---------- | ------ |
| 60 mL | rum   |
| 40 mL | grapefruit juice |
| 15 mL | lime juice |
| 15 mL | maraschino liqueur |

### Method

1. Shake all ingredients over ice.
2. Strain into a coupe glass over crushed ice. Garnish with a lime.


- **Mix**: Shake
- **Glass**: Coupe
- **Over Ice**: Yes
- **Garnish**: Lime

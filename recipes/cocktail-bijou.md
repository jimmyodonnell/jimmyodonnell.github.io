### Ingredients

| ---------- | ------ |
| 45 mL | gin  |
| 30 mL | sweet vermouth (pref. Carpano Antica) |
| 22.5 mL | green Chartreuse |
| 2 dashes | orange bitters |


### Method

1. Stir all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into a cocktail glass.


- **Mix**: Stir
- **Glass**: Cocktail
- **Over Ice**: No
- **Garnish**: None
